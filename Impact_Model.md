# AutoFlow: Impact Model & Business Value

## The Problem
Traditional vendor onboarding requires 4 distinct human touchpoints: Data entry, Background screening, Compliance review, and ERP creation. This process takes an average of **12 to 14 days** per vendor, causing supply chain delays and operational bottlenecks.

## Quantitative Impact Estimate
Based on an enterprise onboarding 1,000 new vendors per year:

### 1. Time Saved
- **Before:** 4 human touchpoints = ~6 hours of active manual work per vendor.
- **With AutoFlow:** 85% of vendors follow the "happy path" requiring 0 human hours. 15% require exception handling (15 mins each).
- **Impact:** Total manual hours reduced from 6,000 hours/year to just 37.5 hours/year. **(99% reduction in manual processing time)**.

### 2. Cost Reduction
- Assuming a blended burdened rate of ₹1,000/hour for compliance and data-entry staff.
- **Current Cost:** 6,000 hours * ₹1,000 = ₹6,000,000 / year.
- **AutoFlow Cost:** 38 hours * ₹1,000 = ₹38,000 / year.
- **Gross Savings:** ~₹59.6 Lakhs annually.

### 3. Cycle Time & Revenue Recovery
- **Cycle Time Reduction:** Onboarding time shrinks from 14 days to **under 3 minutes**.
- **Revenue Impact:** Faster vendor onboarding accelerates time-to-market for products/services, potentially recovering millions in delayed revenue realization and preventing SLA penalty breaches.

*Assumptions: Standard onboarding metrics typical of mid-to-large enterprises in financial services or supply chain in India.*
